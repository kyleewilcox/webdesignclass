About Us

We are staffed by trainers educated in Animal Science and certified by CCPDT and more. Our professional trainers consistently look for ways to improve and broaden their methods to better meet you and your dogs’ needs!

Our Mission Statement

We empower dog parents with the knowledge and confidence needed to enrich and strengthen the relationship with their dogs in a way that allows for stress-free days and promotes positive reinforcement, support, and compassion.

★

Our Vision Statement

Our vision is to foster a community where dog parents feel confident in their relationship with their dogs and where dogs feel supported, celebrated, and loved by their parents.
